Custom sizes or wood species?

If you're interested in a piece you saw here but want it in a different size, a different wood, or have something else in mind entirely — mention that when you reach out. I can't promise I'll take every request, but I'm happy to talk it over. The more detail you give me (dimensions, wood preference, how you plan to use it), the better I can tell you whether it's something I'd build.
